Stand Up for Him or Her

Advocating for your friend with autism is crucial. Stand up for them in situations where they may be misunderstood or treated unfairly.

  • If you witness bullying or exclusion, speak up and support your friend.
  • Educate others about autism and encourage them to be more understanding and inclusive.
  • Be a positive role model by showing kindness and respect towards your friend.
  • Support your friend in expressing their needs and preferences.
