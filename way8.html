Don't Be Afraid

It's natural to feel unsure or anxious about interacting with someone who has autism, but don't let fear hold you back. Approach the friendship with an open heart and mind.

  • Educate yourself about autism to better understand your friend's behaviors and needs.
  • Ask questions and seek guidance from those who have more experience if you're unsure about something.
  • Be patient with yourself as you learn and grow in the friendship.
  • Remember that your friend values your effort and kindness, even if they may not always express it.
