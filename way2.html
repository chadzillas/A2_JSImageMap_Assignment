Find Common Interests

Discover shared activities or hobbies that you and your friend with autism can enjoy together. Finding common interests can help build a stronger connection and make your friend feel more included and valued.

  • Take the time to learn about what your friend enjoys. Ask questions and show genuine interest in their hobbies.
  • Participate in activities that you both find enjoyable. This can range from playing games, sports, or even engaging in creative projects together.
  • Encourage and support your friend's interests. Attend events or activities that they are passionate about.
  • Remember, building a friendship is about sharing experiences and creating positive memories together.
