Give Feedback

Providing constructive feedback can help your friend with autism understand social cues and improve their interactions.

  • Offer positive reinforcement when your friend does something well. Praise their efforts and achievements.
  • Gently guide them when they make mistakes. Explain what went wrong and how they can improve.
  • Be specific in your feedback. Use examples to help them understand better.
  • Encourage self-reflection and self-awareness in your friend.
